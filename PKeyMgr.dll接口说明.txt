证书包工具，根据列表选择需要安装的证书集合。包含两个界面控件。证书安装及证书转换.

使用说明:

vb.net:
        Imports PKeyMgr
        Private WithEvents licenseform As PKeyMgr.Form1
        licenseform = New PKeyMgr.Form1("证书安装卸载")
        licenseform.ShowDialog()

        '版本转换
        Private WithEvents conversionform As PKeyMgr.Form2
        conversionform = New PKeyMgr.Form2()
        conversionform.ShowDialog()
